Research Applications#

PhaseLab supports several active research areas.

Smith-Magenis Syndrome Gene Therapy#

PhaseLab was originally developed for SMS gene therapy research:

  • RAI1 haploinsufficiency causes circadian disruption

  • CRISPRa can restore RAI1 expression

  • Coherence metrics predict therapeutic efficacy

Key publications and resources:

  • SMS Foundation: www.smsfoundation.org

  • RAI1 gene: NCBI Gene ID 10743

Protein Folding Coherence#

Phase coherence correlates with protein folding reliability:

  • High coherence proteins fold consistently

  • Low coherence indicates folding instability

  • Applications in protein engineering

Chronotherapy Optimization#

Circadian-aware drug dosing:

  • Multi-tissue circadian models

  • Optimal dosing schedules

  • AAV delivery timing
